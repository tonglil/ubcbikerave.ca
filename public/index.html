It's The UBC BikeRave 3.0!

Friday, March 27st @ 8pm at Koerner Plaza (in front of Koerner Library)

BikeRave! get the details

bikerave (\bahykreyv\): boisterous conglomeration

def'n: badass swippersnappers gettin' their grunge hipster on to heavy bass, neon lights, and fast bikes.

Bedazzle your wheels, deck out your helmets, put on your craziest outfit, and join us for BikeRave 3.0 – a cycle around campus complete with sick tunes and dance parties.

Thought up in the dark corners of first year residence, the BikeRave is back this year with more bikes, louder jams, and brighter colours. Let’s get turnt up, ya crazy kids.

For Your Safety. be safe kids

Fact: biking is radical.

Fact: raves are radical.

Fact: you are radical, and we don't want the radical you to be hurt.

Please wear a helmet, bring water to stay hydrated, and ride at a slow pace, paying mind to other bikers around you!
